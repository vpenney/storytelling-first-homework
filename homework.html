Baby-sized scale reference

There are a lot of scales in D3, and of course the documentation sucks so this might be enough for now.

Scale Inputs Typical outputs Example
scaleLinear() Numbers Numbers or color range Age of a pandas bear becomes length of a bar
Higher pollution levels become a greener dot
scaleBand() Categories Bar position/size Spacing out bars based on names of senators
scaleOrdinal() Categories Colors (list of specific ones) Republicans are red
Democrats are blue
scaleSqrt() Numbers Radius of circle Age of a tree becomes size of a circle
scalePoint() Categories Evenly spaced out It's like a scaleBand, but for spacing out points/circles instead of bars

Remember, all scales want a domain and a range!

...except scaleOrdinal, but you aren't supposed to know that yet.
